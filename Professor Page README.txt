When the users log in as professors, they will see the professors' page.
There is a calendar to show the dates and also the to do list.
There is a bell icon to show the cases alert
They can view their profiles and the rating students give to them.

When they click the stars, they will be directed to the rating and comment page.
The page have a overall star points and it also shows the students' rating and feedbacks provided to the professor.

When they click this button, they will see the students' status and data page.
This page will have a pie chart shows how many percentage of students have finished their jobs.
And below is the corresponding student list, the professor can click the student's name and heading to the stduent page.

In the student page, the professor can see the status of student, assign the student something to do and 
write suggestions and comments to the student
